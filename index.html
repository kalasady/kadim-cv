About

Kadim J. Al Asady

Principal and Senior Computational Designer with 15+ years of experience across high-rise, sports, healthcare, and civic sectors. Specializes in guiding the geometric and structural logic of complex building systems, developing custom automation tools for design, structural analysis, and documentation workflows, and leading multi-disciplinary engineering teams.

Track record includes landmark NFL and MLS stadiums, international supertall towers, and pioneering academic contributions at major research universities. Founder of PaperSpace.INK, a residential design-build practice. Adjunct Professor at the University of Kansas and Arizona State University's Herberger Institute.

Structural Systems

Guiding the geometric and structural logic of complex building systems — from supertall towers to large-span sports venues — from concept through construction.

Architectural Design

Delivering high-quality architectural design across sports, civic, and residential typologies — including design-build and off-grid construction projects.

Digital Fabrication
